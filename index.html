About Me

I'm a B.Tech Computer Engineering student at SSVPS B.S. Deore College of Engineering, Dhule, passionate about building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems using AI.


Proficient in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, Generative AI, Agentic AI, LLM Fine-Tuning, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), and Computer Vision. I work with Python, Java, JavaScript, and SQL, leveraging frameworks like Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, LangChain, LangGraph, Flask, and ReactJs to build end-to-end AI solutions.


My projects include Kisan AI — an ML-based crop recommendation and CNN-powered disease detection platform, AuraLearn — an AI educational assistant (Rank 8 at IIT Madras among 1000+ teams), and a Skin Cancer Detection CNN classifier on the HAM10000 dataset.


Currently exploring LLM Fine-Tuning (LoRA/QLoRA), Vector Databases, Multi-Agent Systems, and MLOps Pipelines (MLflow, DVC). As Vice President of the Brocode Coding Club, I lead technical events, hackathons, and coding workshops for 200+ students.


🎓 Certified by Microsoft & LinkedIn (Career Essentials in Data Analysis), IIT Bombay (Python & Java Training), and IIT Guwahati (Time Series Analytics).


📩 Open to internships, entry-level roles, and opportunities in AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science.
